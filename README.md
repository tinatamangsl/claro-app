## The Solution

**Claro** is a voice-first cognitive clarity companion for ambitious, multi-passionate professionals.

It calls your phone during your commute and reads your **top 3 priorities aloud** — so you can go from sleep to peak performance without opening a single app.

### 3. 🎤 Creative Use of ClawdTalk Technology

Claro doesn’t just *use* ClawdTalk — it makes ClawdTalk the **core magic** of the product.

**The innovation: Bidirectional voice task management on a commute.**

Here’s how it works:

1. **Telnyx calls Natasha’s phone** as she boards the Overground
2. **Resemble AI’s Lucy voice** reads her top 3 priorities in a warm, personalised tone
3. Natasha speaks back: *"Move contractor call to tomorrow"*
4. **ClawdTalk transcribes her reply** in real time
5. **ClawHouse AI agent** processes the intent and reschedules the task
6. Natasha arrives at work. Her task list is already updated. She opened zero apps.

This is the **first demo of bidirectional voice task management during a commute** — a genuinely novel interaction pattern.

**Additional creative ClawdTalk integrations:**
- "Calls by Telnyx · Voice by ClawdTalk" — clearly badged on every audio interaction
- ClawHouse AI agent floating on every page — ask questions, update tasks, get briefings by voice or chat
- Resemble AI Chatterbox TTS — Lucy voice persona, clearly attributed in the UI with animated waveform

## 🛠️ How All 4 Sponsors Work Together

Claro is the **only app in this hackathon that chains all 4 sponsor tools into a single user journey:**

```
Telnyx (calls phone) → Resemble AI (speaks priorities) → ClawdTalk (hears reply) → ClawHouse (processes & updates tasks)
```

| Sponsor | Creative Integration | Where You See It |
|---|---|---|
| **Telnyx** | Outbound phone call triggers the entire commute briefing flow | Dashboard + Commute page, “Tap to get called via Telnyx” button |
| **Resemble AI Chatterbox** | Lucy voice persona — warm, personalised TTS in 6 languages | Commute page waveform, “Powered by Resemble AI” badge, live audio |
| **ClawdTalk** | Two-way voice reply — Natasha speaks back and tasks update | Commute page, “Voice by ClawdTalk” footer badge |
| **ClawHouse** | Persistent AI agent on every page — voice + chat interface | Floating “Ask Claro” button, bottom-right, every page |
